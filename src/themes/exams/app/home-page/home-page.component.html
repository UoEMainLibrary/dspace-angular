The very latest papers added are from the 2023-24 diet. These pages will be updated periodically, as more papers become available. If you cannot find the paper you are looking for, please email exam.papers@ed.ac.uk, or try visiting again at a later date.

Search Tips
  • Typing in the course name or code will bring up suggestions for records already in the database.
  • If you cannot find the paper you are looking for from a title search try entering the course code. Course codes can change over time. Click the 'All papers for this course' link in your search results to get full results for the course title.
  • You can browse to the paper you are looking for by clicking the options on the right-hand side of the screen to narrow the results by School, subject, year and title.
Marking Guides and Sample Solutions

The Library does not hold any marking guides or sample solutions to exam papers. We suggest that you contact the course secretary or course organiser to check if these have been created for your course and how they have been made available.
